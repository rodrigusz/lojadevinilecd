    1989 (Taylor's Version) contains a total of 21 songs, 5 of which are previously unreleased songs from The Vault. All of the classic songs from the album have been given a Taylor makeover and are presented here in her own unique vision. 1989 features some of Taylor's biggest pop songs, including "Shake It Off", "Blank Space" and "Bad Blood" + several other absolute bangers.
