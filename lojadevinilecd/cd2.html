    PARAMORE


    2022 saw Paramore quietly updating the artwork of their Self-Titled album across all digital services, transitioning from a group photo to a solo image of Hayley donning her "Grow Up" denim jacket. This change sparked an enthusiastic response from fans, demanding the record label to release the album on vinyl featuring the updated cover art. The prayers have been heard! On January 5th 2024, the Self-Titled album will be available once again, this time pressed on a striking tangerine 2LP colored vinyl with the Denim Jacket artwork. What a way to kick off the new year!
